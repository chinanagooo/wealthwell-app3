# Finora

## Overview

Finora is a financial intelligence app that helps investors understand their overall financial health. Today, many people manage money across several platforms such as banks, investment apps, crypto exchanges, and property records. As a result, wealth often becomes fragmented, making it difficult to see one’s true financial position.

## Unified Wealth Dashboard

To address this, Finora brings everything into one unified dashboard. By aggregating assets, liabilities, and income from different sources, the platform provides users with a clear view of their net worth, portfolio allocation, and key financial metrics in one place. In addition, Finora supports multiple asset types including cash, stocks, property, and digital assets, allowing investors to see their complete financial picture across platforms.

## User Modes

The app is also designed for different users. Investor Mode presents insights in a simple and intuitive format so individuals can quickly understand their finances, while Pro Mode offers deeper analytics for users who want a more advanced view of their portfolio and risk exposure.

## Financial Resilience

What makes Finora unique is its focus on financial resilience rather than simple wealth tracking. By combining unified wealth visibility with resilience analysis and scenario testing, Finora transforms complex financial data into practical guidance, making it a cohesive wealth hub.
